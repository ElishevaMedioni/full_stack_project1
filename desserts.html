    • Lemon Pie

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup sugar

    • 1/4 cup cornstarch

    • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

    • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    • 2 cups water

    • 3 large egg beaten

    • 1 tablespoon butter

    • 1/4 cup lemon juice

    • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest

    • 1 pastry shell (9 inches), baked

    For the meringue

    • 3 large egg whites, room temperature

    • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    • 1/2 cup sugar

    Receipe

    • In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, flour and salt. Gradually stir in water. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly. Reduce heat; cook and stir 2 minutes more. Remove from the heat. Gradually stir 1 cup of hot mixture into egg yolks; return all to saucepan. Bring to boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat. Stir in the butter, lemon juice and zest until smooth. Pour into pastry shell.

    • In a bowl, beat egg whites and salt until stiff (but not dry) peaks form. Gradually beat in sugar until soft peaks form. Spread over pie, sealing edges to pastry. Bake at 350Â° until meringue is golden, 12-15 minutes. Cool. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator.

    Chocolate Cake

    Ingredients

    • 170 gr of dark chocolate

    • 3 eggs

    • 70 gr canola oil

    • 50 gr flour

    • 80 gr sugar

    Receipe

    • Melt the chocolate with one table of oil.

    • Mix all the ingredients together.

    • Bake it for 15 minutes in a 18o degrees celcius oven.

    Lotus ice cream

    Ingredients

    • 6 eggs

    • half a cup of sugar

    • 370 gr of cream

    • 3 table spoons of lotus paste

    Receipe

    • Separate the egg yolks from the whites. Beat the whites.

    • Mix the yolks with the sugar. Add the whites softly.

    • Beat the cream and add the lotus paste. Add it softly.

    • Put it in the freezer for a night.
